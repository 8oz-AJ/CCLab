Habitat:

Physarums usually live alone until mating season starts which is around spring and is considered an adult from birth. The creature itself does not age and can only die when a natural predator kills it. It lives in a dark and humid environment so when thunderstorms happen they are ready to collect the energy leftover by lightning strikes.

Appearance:

What we know so far is that they are green mostly and yellow during mating season. They multiply based on the amount they consume and they are still an enigmatic creature.

Key Characteristics:

  • Moves randomly
  • Only moves when lightning strikes
  • Never alone
  • No organs so far
